== Technical implementation of application framework: drafting and evaluation of resource requirements ==

=== Description of Deliverable ===

 * Implementation draft of the application architecture (untested, incomplete). Choice of technologies on top of C#.NET to minimize the development costs by re-using prior art.
 * Evaluation of development costs for the required Urakawa SDK (data model and API) increments to support full-text full-audio.
 * Estimated resource plan to ensure completion of both core and GUI implementations (to be submitted to the board for decision about staff allocation).
