[A] Requirements gathering, Usability and Workflow (second part, continued from previous phase)

Description of Deliverable

Continuation (improvement and completion) of the previous phase's deliverables:

  • Graphical User Interface (GUI) mockups: images with accessible descriptions (screenshots and/or montages).
